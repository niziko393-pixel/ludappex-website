Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Ludappex LLC ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use Stack Builder Pro (the "Game"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. Please read this policy carefully.

1. Information We Collect

Stack Builder Pro does not directly collect or store any personally identifiable information. However, the third-party services integrated into the Game may collect certain data automatically:

  • Device information – device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage data – gameplay session duration, interactions within the Game
  • Advertising data – ad impressions, clicks, and interaction data collected by Google AdMob

We store the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your best score
  • Selected game skins and preferences
  • Selected language

2. How We Use Information

We use the information collected solely to:

  • Provide and improve the Game experience
  • Display advertisements through Google AdMob (including rewarded ads)
  • Analyze gameplay to fix bugs and improve performance

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Stack Builder Pro uses Google AdMob to display banner and rewarded video advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized ads based on your interests.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Children's Privacy

Stack Builder Pro is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. All game preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your personal data to our servers.

6. Third-Party Services

Ostrich vs Monkey — Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025  |  Developer: Ludappex LLC

This Privacy Policy describes how Ludappex LLC ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Ostrich vs Monkey mobile game ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect any personal information. However, the third-party services integrated into the App may collect certain data as described below.

2. Third-Party Services

Google AdMob Ads

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the App. AdMob may collect and use data to show personalized or non-personalized ads. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Device type, OS version
  • App usage and interaction data

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Play Services Platform

The App runs on Android and uses Google Play Services. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

3. Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your highest score (best score)
  • Game preferences

This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

4. Children's Privacy

Ostrich vs Monkey is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App does not require account registration, login, or any personal data input from users.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information within the App. Since we do not collect or transmit personal data ourselves, the primary security responsibility lies with third-party service providers (Google AdMob).

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

7. Your Rights

You can manage your ad preferences through:

  • Android Settings → Google → Ads → Reset Advertising ID or opt out
  • Google Ad Settings

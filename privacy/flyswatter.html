🪰 Fly Swatter Frenzy — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2026

Ludappex LLC ("we", "our", or "us") built Fly Swatter Frenzy as a free, ad-supported game. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Fly Swatter Frenzy does not require user registration or login. We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address.

The app stores game progress (level, score, settings, and language preference) locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

Google AdMob — We display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and usage data to serve relevant ads. For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Play Services — Required for app distribution and updates.

Advertising

Fly Swatter Frenzy is a free app supported by advertisements. We use the following ad formats:

• Banner ads — displayed at the bottom of the screen

• Interstitial ads — displayed between levels (infrequently)

• Rewarded ads — optional ads that the user chooses to watch in exchange for in-game benefits

We do not use aggressive or deceptive advertising practices. All full-screen ads have a clearly visible close button.

Children's Privacy

Fly Swatter Frenzy is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal data to function. Ad content is filtered to be appropriate for all audiences.

Data Storage

All game data (scores, progress, preferences) is stored locally on your device. We do not operate any servers or databases that store user information. Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data.

Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

• Internet access — Required to display advertisements

• Network state — Used to check connectivity for ad loading

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
